1. Personal Information

Enter your full name in the "Name" field. Example: Won Bin

Enter your IC number in the "IC Number" field. Example: 123456-78-9101

Select your gender using the radio buttons given.

Enter your date of birth in the "Date of Birth" field. Example: 2000-01-01

2. Education Background

Select your gender using the radio buttons given.

Enter your date of birth in the "Date of Birth" field. Example: 2000-01-01

Enter your ethnicity and religion in the respective fields.

If you have OKU status, enter the status and OKU card number.

3. Subject Selection

Select the subjects you want to register for by checking the corresponding checkboxes.

Compulsory Subjects: Select at least one compulsory subject.

Elective Subjects: Select optional elective subjects based on your preferences.
